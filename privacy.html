Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At Glotix, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered dubbing, text-to-speech, and speech-to-text services.

1 Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and profile picture from your sign-in provider (Google, GitHub, etc.).
  • Uploaded media: Audio and video files you upload for dubbing, transcription, or voice cloning.
  • Generated content: Translated audio, subtitles, and synthesized voices created by our AI.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about feature usage to improve the platform.

2 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your dubbing, TTS, and STT requests.
  • Storing your generated files so you can access them later.
  • Managing your account and credit balance.
  • Improving our AI models (only with explicit opt-in consent).

We never sell your data to third parties. Your uploaded files are processed by our AI workers and stored privately in your account.

3 Data Storage & Security

Your files are stored on encrypted servers hosted by Cloudflare R2 with industry-standard security:

  • All connections use HTTPS / TLS encryption.
  • Files are accessible only through signed URLs tied to your account.
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase using OAuth 2.0.
  • Generated files are automatically deleted after 7 days unless saved to your library.

4 Third-Party Services

Glotix uses the following trusted services to operate:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database.
  • Cloudflare R2 — File storage.
  • Modal — GPU compute for AI processing.
  • Anthropic Claude — AI translation engine.

5 Payments

If you purchase credits, payment is processed by secure third-party providers. We do not store credit card information — all sensitive payment data is handled directly by the payment processor.

6 Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7 Cookies

Glotix uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8 Children's Privacy

Glotix is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced via email or a notice on the site. Continued use of Glotix after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10 Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or data requests, please reach out to us:

  • Email: support@glotix.ai
  • Privacy inquiries: privacy@glotix.ai
