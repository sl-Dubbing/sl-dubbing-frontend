How it works

Professional AI dubbing — from one upload to global reach

Dubbing Studio turns any video or audio into a multilingual production. Upload your file, set the source language and one or more target languages, then Glotix separates vocals, translates the script, synthesizes natural speech (with optional voice clone), and mixes the new voice back with your original background music and effects.

Smart translation

Regional AI translation adapts tone and dialect — not word-for-word subtitles.

Voice clone

Clone the original speaker from your upload, or use Premium voices / saved samples.

Mix preserved

Background music and sound effects stay intact while vocals are replaced.
